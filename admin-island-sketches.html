Island sketch taper

Hand-drawn topographies in design/mascots/sketch_v0..v9.png run edge-to-edge — they read as ground strips, not islands. This page pulls the topline down at the left/right edges so each silhouette terminates at the bottom corners — matching how run_island_v0_APPROVED.png ends. Hill positions and the topline shape in the middle are preserved. Tweak the slider, then download the corrected PNGs and drop them next to the originals as sketch_v0_island.png … sketch_v9_island.png.
