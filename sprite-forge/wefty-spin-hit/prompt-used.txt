Panel-by-panel — 6-frame spin attack
────────────────────────────────────────

PANEL 1 — Anticipation (wind-up)
Wefty crouches slightly (body squashed vertically 8–10%).
Eyes focused, mouth determined / small grin.
Yarn tail pulled slightly backward like tension.
Subtle lean opposite spin direction (prep pose).

PANEL 2 — Launch
Body unsquashes into slight stretch (height +6%).
Face begins rotating off-center (about 20–30° turn cue).
Add short curved motion streaks around lower sides.
Tail begins trailing behind spin direction.

PANEL 3 — Fast Spin (smear A)
Ball appears partially motion-blurred in a circular arc.
Face details simplified / partially obscured by speed.
Yarn bands form curved streak impression (not random redraw).
Add stronger circular speed lines wrapping around body.

PANEL 4 — Impact Peak
Most aggressive frame: exaggerated spin blur ring.
Tiny star / spark hit accent at one edge (enemy contact cue).
Body slightly squashed on contact side (impact compression).
Expression intense (eyes narrowed or determined).

PANEL 5 — Deceleration (smear B)
Blur reduces; face becomes readable again at opposite angle.
Speed lines thinner / shorter than panel 4.
Tail whips past body, showing follow-through.
Body returns toward normal proportions.

PANEL 6 — Recovery / Ready
Wefty returns to clean idle-like shape, tiny bounce settle.
Confident face (small smile), ready for next input.
Tail settles downward with slight curve.
No speed lines (or only faint residual arc).
